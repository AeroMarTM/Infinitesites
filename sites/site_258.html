A desert road stretches its patience thin
Heat edits the air
a pause that overstayed





the smell of old books winning


an apology rehearsed too well

bookmark lost, story fine







A bicycle bell clears a small path
Courtesy travels faster
background noise becoming foreground
a pause that helped

the page accepted it







light flickered
so did doubt
a typo changed the tone

the note said “later”














A baker greets dough like a friend
Yeast does the talking

the bus sighed with us

lines crossed
connection lost

waiting room philosophy


sentence found its ending

a map folded the wrong way


A café window frames a thousand small lives
Each cup cools at its own pace

light flickered
so did doubt

expectation revised downward


morning light arguing with curtains

breadcrumb trail of unread messages


note to self: check pockets twice

coffee ring marking history
