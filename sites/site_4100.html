Clouded Pine

typo survived three rereads

words queued politely
A lighthouse blinks to no one in particular
Habit keeps it honest

A desert road stretches its patience thin
Heat edits the air


rain practicing patience on glass

road noise thinking out loud

clouds pretending to be decisive

chair by the window waiting


the page accepted it

a whisper doing heavy lifting

A city exhales after midnight
Lights dim their opinions


the pause felt earned

note faded but stayed







waiting ended without warning





sentence found its ending

shoes remembered the path


optimism with a scratch

A carpenter squares a stubborn board
Wood remembers forests
attention wandered responsibly the lamp leaned in

weather finally decided


weather matching the mood

patience learning to stretch


the bench remembered everyone







Chalk dust floats after a solved problem
Answers feel provisional

the clock behaved
a song stuck on one line

a map folded the wrong way


coffee reheated, soul not
